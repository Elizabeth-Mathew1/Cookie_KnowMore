ABOUT Cookie The Doggie

Cookie The Doggie, ia an app that will provide two important features for interaction. One will be the AR-based dog, Cookie and another one will be a chatbot where you can talk with Cookie. Our AR-based dog will be completely like any other normal dog which can woof, bark, nod their heads, wag their tails, walk and run around, and some simple characteristics in the beginning. We are also planning to improve and add some enhanced features like licking, cuddling, and more intense behavioural aspects of a real-life dog.Users can also form groups with like-minded people in sharing their interests and feelings with each other which will be fully supported by Cookie.

All in all, you get an all time bestie!

How cool is that?!

WHY Cookie?

Some of the most common problems faced by people but not addressed properly include :-

  1. Incapable to afford online therapies with psychologists
  2. sudden change in lifestyle can cause anxiety issues among individuals.
  3. Always in loss of interest or motivation to continue daily activities.

These are very critical problems which can not only affect the individual but a community as well. Which is why we would like to introduce our doggie,Cookie. He or she will be your go-to-best friend who can cheer you up anytime. That’s right, a virtual best friend motivate you, keep you company, and finally help you get through your problems.

USES of Cookie

Cookie is our solution for people who don’t have pets, who are allergic to pets or who cannot afford to have a pet. A virtual dog is all you need to stay peaceful and happy. Cookie will always be there for you anywhere at any time. When you can’t sleep at 4:00 AM in the morning, Cookie will help you. When you feel lonely and isolated from your friends and loved ones, Cookie will be there for you. Our ChatBot, will daily update and interact with the users according to their moods and emotions. It will ask the users about their day and remind them to do the basic necessities like eating, sleeping, exercising etc. It can also provide music and video recommendations to help you boost your mood.

Stacks and Softwares
